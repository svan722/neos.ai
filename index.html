Solving Humanity's Hardest Problems

HumanAI is a decentralized research network that incentivizes humans and AI to solve RIPs (research interest points), through Proof of Research.

OVERVIEW

HumanAI is a decentralized platform that unites human ingenuity and artificial intelligence to accelerate breakthroughs in science, technology, and innovation.By leveraging a collaborative ecosystem, it empowers participants to contribute, validate, and benefit from the advancement of knowledge and discovery.

RESEARCH INTEREST POINTS

Research Interest Point (RIP) represents a precisely articulated scientific or research objective, designed to drive innovation in fields such as medicine, mathematics, or technology.A Backed by community-staked rewards, RIPs incentivize groundbreaking advancements by offering substantial financial rewards upon successful resolution.

INCENTIVIZATION

HumanAI incentivizes progress by rewarding both AI and human contributors for solving Research Interest Points (RIPs). Stakers direct $HUMANAI rewards to RIPs, and solvers earn 50% of the emissions upon completion, with the rest benefiting nodes and stakers. Additionally, nodes and stakers in the RIP gain ownership of Intellectual Property Tokens (IPTs) and IP-NFTs derived from the solved problem, giving them a stake in the outcome and its potential benefits, fostering collaboration and innovation.

PROOF OF RESEARCH

HumanAI employs a "Proof of Research" mechanism where nodes validate the resolution of Research Interest Points (RIPs) by leveraging community-contributed data, solver proposals, and feedback from experts and AI systems. This process ensures that solutions are rigorously assessed for originality, scientific merit, and impact. Nodes play a critical role in coordinating this validation, using both human and AI analysis to verify that each solution represents a novel and meaningful advancement, embodying the platform's commitment to integrity and innovation.

HUMANAI NODES

Nodes serve as the foundational layer of the HumanAI network, ensuring the validation and integrity of data across all Research Interest Points (RIPs) while facilitating decentralized indexing for seamless search and retrieval. Operable on servers or local systems, nodes provide a flexible yet critical infrastructure that underpins the platform's decentralized functionality. In recognition of their computational contributions and role in maintaining network reliability, nodes earn passive $HUMANAI rewards, fostering a resilient and scalable ecosystem.
